Things to Remember

  • Weather:Calgary's weather can change quickly. Pack layers and be prepared for sudden shifts.
  • Transportation: The city has excellent public transport, including buses and the CTrain. Walking and biking are also popular in some areas.
  • Events:Don’t miss the Calgary Stampede if you’re visiting in July—it's the "Greatest Outdoor Show on Earth."
  • Currency:The currency used in Calgary is the Canadian Dollar (CAD).
  • Emergency Numbers: Dial 911 for police, fire, or medical emergencies.
  • Local Etiquette: Canadians are friendly and polite. A simple "please" and "thank you" go a long way!
